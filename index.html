Delete Account - Snippit

You can request deletion of your Snippit account and associated data in one of the following ways:

  • From inside the app via the Delete Account option in account settings
  • By emailing furkanyoruk.dev@gmail.com from your registered email address

When your request is confirmed, your account and associated personal data will be deleted within 7 days, unless retention is required for legal or security purposes.

If you have any questions, contact: furkanyoruk.dev@gmail.com
